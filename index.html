Difference between Insurance and Reinsurance

Basis of comparison Insurance Reinsurance
Meaning Insurance alludes to a contract between two parties wherein one party promises to idemnigyyy the other in case of loss or death. Reinsurance means insurance taken up by an insurance company when it does not wish to bear entire risk of loss and thus shares it with some other insurer.
Protection Provided to individual or things. Taken by the large insurance companies to survive large losses.
Premium Paid by an individual, is received by an insurance company. Paid for reinsurance is divided among the insurance companies in specified ratio.
